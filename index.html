Your Friends

Rachel Green

The Rich Brat

Phoebe Buffay

The Weird Girl

Monica Geller

The Clean Freak

Bossy and a cleanliness-freak. Dominant over Chandler as her spouse. Earlier was fat as a potato. Very competitive in nature.
